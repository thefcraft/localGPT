# Mathematical Expressions and Formatting Example

## Basic Math Operations
- Addition: \(a + b\)
- Subtraction: \(a - b\)
- Multiplication: \(a \times b\) or \(a \cdot b\)
- Division: \(\frac{a}{b}\) or \(a \div b\)

## Advanced Math
- Square Root: \(\sqrt{a}\)
- Cube Root: \(\sqrt[3]{a}\)
- Summation: \(\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i\)
- Product: \(\prod_{i=1}^{n} b_i\)

## Trigonometry
- Sine Function: \(\sin(\theta)\)
- Cosine Function: \(\cos(\theta)\)
- Tangent Function: \(\tan(\theta)\)

## Algebraic Expressions
- Quadratic Formula: \(x = \frac{{-b \pm \sqrt{{b^2-4ac}}}}{{2a}}\)
- Binomial Coefficient: \({n \choose k}\)

## Limits and Derivatives
- Limit: \(\lim_{{x \to a}} f(x)\)
- Derivative: \(\frac{{df(x)}}{{dx}}\) or \(f'(x)\)

## Matrix Operations
- Matrix Multiplication: \(A \cdot B\)
- Determinant: \(|A|\)
- Transposition: \(A^T\)

## Statistics
- Mean: \(\mu = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i\)
- Standard Deviation: \(\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} (x_i - \mu)^2}\)

## Probability
- Probability: \(P(A)\)
- Conditional Probability: \(P(A | B)\)

## Formatting
- **Bold Text**: **This is bold text.**
- *Italic Text*: *This is italic text.*
- ~~Strikethrough Text~~: ~~This is strikethrough text.~~
- Inline Code: `print("Hello, World!")`

## Equations
- Quadratic Equation:
  \[ ax^2 + bx + c = 0 \]

- Pythagorean Theorem:
  \[ c = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2} \]
